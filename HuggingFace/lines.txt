Stockholm is the capital of Sweden and is widely known for its stunning architecture, clean streets, and high quality of life. The city is spread across several islands, connected by an extensive network of bridges and ferries, making transportation both unique and efficient. In recent years, Stockholm has also emerged as a major technology hub in Europe, attracting startups and global companies alike. This growth has been supported by strong government policies and a highly skilled workforce. However, the rapid expansion of the tech sector has also raised concerns about housing shortages and increasing living costs.
